MTG clone, lands on terrain, creatures on board
Turnbased 

1. Deckbuilder

2. Game Map
    -Generate mana
    -Place lands
    -Place creatures on home row
    -Play sorceries/enchants
    -Creatures all move forwards by movement value in their lane
    -Creatures attack if they hit something in their lane, home row arrival results in player damage or land pillaged
    -Creatures that have died are removed
    -Creatures that have reached the home row are reset to their origin

3. Campaign Map
    -Dueling house -> next opponent battle
    -Spell shop (spend tp)
    -Builder's library (manage decks)
